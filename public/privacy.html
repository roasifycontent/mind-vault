Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 2025  ·  Last updated: March 2025

Mindspan ("we", "our", "the app") is a daily memory training application. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We believe in plain language — no legal jargon.

What We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Email address — used to identify your account. Never shared or used for marketing.
  • Password — stored as a one-way cryptographic hash (bcrypt, 12 rounds). We cannot read your password.
  • Game statistics — your scores, best score, games played, and current streak. This is the core data that makes the app useful.

We do not collect your name, location, device identifiers, browsing history, or any data beyond what is listed above.

How We Use Your Data

  • To create and authenticate your account
  • To sync your progress across devices
  • To display your training history within the app

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's functionality.

Data Storage

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Neon (neon.tech), a serverless database provider. The app is served via Vercel's infrastructure. Both services are based in the United States and comply with standard data protection practices.

Your data is transmitted over HTTPS at all times.

Authentication Tokens

When you sign in, we issue a JWT (JSON Web Token) that expires after 30 days. This token is stored locally on your device (localStorage) and is used to authenticate your requests. It is never sent to third parties.

Third Parties

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third parties. The only external services involved are:

  • Neon — database hosting (your stats and hashed credentials)
  • Vercel — app hosting and serverless functions
  • Google Fonts — typography (loaded from Google's CDN; subject to Google's privacy policy)

Data Retention

Your account and statistics are retained for as long as you use the app. If you would like your data deleted, contact us at the address below and we will remove your account and all associated data within 7 days.

Children's Privacy

Mindspan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all data
  • Export your game statistics

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about your privacy or data? Contact us at hello@mindspanapp.com
