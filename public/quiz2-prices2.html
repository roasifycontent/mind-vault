5 warning signs your memory needs attention

Most people dismiss these as "just getting older." But research shows they're early signals — and catching them now could make all the difference.

The "tip of the tongue" moment

You know the word but can't retrieve it. Happening more often than it used to.

2

Walking into rooms blank

You get up to do something, walk there, and have no idea why.

3

Repeating yourself in conversation

You tell someone a story, only to realise you already told them last week.

4

Losing track mid-sentence

"What was I saying?" happens more than you'd like to admit.

5

Names you should know disappearing

Not strangers — people you've known for years. The name just... won't come.
