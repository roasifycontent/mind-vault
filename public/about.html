Our Mission

We believe no one should lose their sharpest years without a fight.

Recall Better exists for one reason: to give every adult over 50 the tools neuroscience says actually work — delivered simply, daily, and without the jargon.

Our Story

The advice was always the same. The tools never were.

"Stay mentally active." That's what every doctor, every article, every well-meaning friend tells you once you pass 50. But no one tells you how.

So most people reach for crosswords or Sudoku. And those are fine — but they only train one narrow type of thinking. Doing the same crossword every morning is like going to the gym and only ever using one machine.

Meanwhile, there are decades of peer-reviewed research showing exactly which kinds of mental exercise actually strengthen memory, processing speed, and reasoning. That research just never made it into something normal people could use.

We built Recall Better to bridge that gap — real cognitive science, delivered in 5 minutes a day, designed for people who want to stay sharp but aren't interested in complicated apps or exaggerated claims.

Our Approach

Three principles behind everything we build

01

Evidence-Aligned

Every game maps directly to a studied cognitive skill. We don't guess what might work. We follow the research — from spaced repetition to adaptive difficulty curves — and design around what the science has consistently shown.

02

Personalised

Your brain isn't the same as anyone else's. Recall Better adapts to your weak areas, adjusts difficulty in real time, and builds a daily plan around where you'll benefit most — not a one-size-fits-all puzzle feed.

03

Effortless

Five minutes a day. No complicated setup. No accounts to manage. Works on any device with a browser. We designed Recall Better for people who value their time and don't want another app that demands hours of attention.
